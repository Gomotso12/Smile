Web Design &
Development
Course

A web design and development course provides essential skills for creating and maintaining websites.
Topics typically include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design, which are crucial for building visually appealing and functional sites. Students also learn about user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design principles to ensure websites are user-friendly.
Advanced courses may cover back-end development with languages like PHP, Python, or Ruby, and frameworks such as Django or Ruby on Rails. Content management systems (CMS) like WordPress are also explored. Practical projects allow students to apply their knowledge and build a portfolio.
